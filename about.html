About Alex Williams

What started as a love of painting became something bigger.

I create hand airbrushed pet portraits - not just to celebrate the animals who mean the world to us, but to turn creativity in to kindness. A portion of every portrait goes toward helping real people in real moments: paying bills, surprising families, supporting those who need it the most.

We document these moments as best we can, honestly - no script, no stunts - just acts of kindness and human contact.

When you commision a portrait, buy a print or just donate your time to watching or sharing our mission, you're not only getting a piece of artwork. You're becoming a part of a story where creativity gives back.

Art has always been a part of my life. I studied drama at university, but my creative journey eventually led me back to my first love—painting. I discovered the incredible art of airbrushing and was fortunate to learn from talented mentors like Simon Murray in Ireland and Dru Blair. Their guidance inspired me to hone my craft and bring life and vibrancy to my portraits.

For years, I balanced a full-time job with my artistic pursuits, painting in my spare time to keep my passion alive. Now, as a parent to a lively and curious little boy, I’ve found even greater motivation to pursue my dream full-time. I want to show my son that it’s possible to follow your passions and, in doing so, make the world a little brighter.

Creating custom pet portraits is more than just painting for me—it’s about celebrating the deep bond people share with their animals. Each piece I create is infused with care, attention to detail, and a genuine love for storytelling through art.

Thank you for visiting my website and considering me to bring your cherished pet to life on canvas. I can’t wait to collaborate with you to create a piece of art that you’ll treasure forever.

It will also give you the joy of knowing that you’re helping to make a positive impact, and spreading kindness in the world.
